RIZE OF DYSTOPIA

By Pepito Perez

High School Freshman | Game Developer

PITCH

After a conflict that eradicated almost all life on Earth, you are made leader to rebuild society. In *Rize Of Dystopia*, you will be managing four factions:

  • The Verzdent – produces food for the people
  • Aquwafz – provides drinkable water
  • MediTex – creates medicine
  • EnerGen – produces energy for all factions to run

Your objective is to ensure each faction receives the resources it needs to survive. Peace must be maintained—neglect even one, and rebellion may arise. Balance, strategy, and foresight are your keys to success.

THEME

*Rize Of Dystopia* embodies the G4C theme of Peace Formers through its focus on post-apocalyptic reconstruction and social balance. The game’s dystopian world is visualized through detailed UI, ruined environments, and somber tones.

Players learn how fragile civilization can be, and how interdependence between factions mirrors the real world’s reliance on sustainable food, water, medicine, and energy. The game also reflects real issues such as freshwater shortages, global food insecurity, and energy dependence.

MY WHY

I created *Rize Of Dystopia* to raise awareness about how interconnected our world truly is. Every society relies on critical resources—and when even one falters, the consequences ripple throughout.

Through this game, I want players to experience the pressure of leadership, and reflect on the challenges of global resource management. Hopefully, it inspires players to think about how they can be part of the solution in the real world.
